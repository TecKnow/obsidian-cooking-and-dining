#  Basmati rice

## Ingredients
- 2 cups basmati rice, not washed
- 2 cups water
## Directions
1.  7 minutes high pressure
2. 10 minutes natural release
3. fluff the rice with the rice scoop
## Source
These are Josh Mueller's instructions for cooking basmati rice in the InstantPot.
